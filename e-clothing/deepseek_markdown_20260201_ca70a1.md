# StyleHub E-commerce Website

A modern, responsive e-commerce website for a fashion retail startup featuring 12 premium clothing items.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Product Catalog**: Display all 12 clothing items with filtering and sorting
- **Shopping Cart**: Add, remove, and update quantities of items
- **User Authentication**: Login and registration system
- **Contact Form**: With validation and FAQ section
- **About Page**: Company story, mission, and team information
- **Product Details**: Modal view with size selection
- **Order Summary**: Calculate totals with tax and shipping

## Pages

1. **Homepage** (`index.html`) - Featured products and company introduction
2. **Products** (`products.html`) - Full product catalog with filtering
3. **About Us** (`about.html`) - Company story, mission, and team
4. **Contact Us** (`contact.html`) - Contact form and company information
5. **Login/Register** (`login.html`) - User authentication
6. **Shopping Cart** (`cart.html`) - Cart management and checkout

## Setup Instructions

1. **Clone or Download** the project files
2. **Open** `index.html` in your browser
3. **No server required** - works as a static website
